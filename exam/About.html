Wesbite Description

According to Malindog-Uy, "Around 7.3 million Filipinos have temporarily or permanently lost their jobs due to business closures because of the pandemic." Ever since the start of the lockdown or quarantine most of Filipinos are struggling to survive. Since people nowadays focused/transfer their day to day basis online, this environment would help begin a new normal in various sectors in the economy. This would help minimize the spread of COVID19 and also continue peoples livelihoods. The solution is to create a platform where people can post their busines, where people can find online jobs, and in addition where people can gain factual information about COVID-19. Simple business ranging from selling plants, food business to employee hiring for companies. A platform that administers the great loss in economy.
